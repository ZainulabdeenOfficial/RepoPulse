About GitHub Project Analyzer

AI-powered insights to help open source developers improve and grow their repositories.

Our Mission

We believe that every open source project has the potential to make a significant impact. Our mission is to provide developers with the tools and insights they need to improve their repositories, attract contributors, and build thriving communities.

Popularity Prediction

Analyze stars, forks, and engagement metrics to predict future growth potential and identify opportunities for improvement.

Maintainer Activity

Track commit frequency, response times, and community engagement to assess project health and maintainer dedication.
